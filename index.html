Our Story

Guardians of the Sungadi Heritage

Thamayanthi Boutique is dedicated exclusively to preserving and promoting the living heritage of Chinalampatti Sungadi sarees and Madurai Sungudi sarees — two of Tamil Nadu's most celebrated GI-tagged handloom textiles. We work directly with master weavers and dyer families from Chinalampatti (Dindigul district) and Madurai to bring you 100% authentic, certified handloom sarees with every order.

The Madurai Sungudi tradition traces back to the 16th century, when Saurashtrian artisans settled in Madurai under the Nayak dynasty and created this iconic tie-and-dye cotton saree inspired by cosmic stars. The Chinalampatti Sungadi evolved alongside — today powering the economy of over 50,000 people in Dindigul and exported to Singapore, Malaysia, Sri Lanka and South Africa.
