Our Story

Guardians of the Sungadi Heritage

✦

Thamayanthi Boutique is dedicated exclusively to preserving and promoting the living heritage of Chinalampatti Sungadi sarees and Madurai Sungudi sarees — two of Tamil Nadu's most celebrated GI-tagged handloom textiles. We work directly with master weavers from Chinalampatti (Dindigul district) and Madurai to bring you 100% authentic, certified handloom sarees.

The Madurai Sungudi tradition traces back to the 16th century, when Saurashtrian artisans settled in Madurai under the Nayak dynasty. The Chinalampatti Sungadi evolved alongside — today powering the economy of over 50,000 people in Dindigul and exported to Singapore, Malaysia, Sri Lanka and South Africa.

16th Century
Saurashtrian artisans migrate to Madurai under Nayak kings — Madurai Sungudi is born.
Pre-Independence Era
Chinalampatti grows to 30,000+ weavers — every household has a loom. The art reaches its peak.
December 2005
Madurai Sungudi receives official GI Tag from Govt. of India — Class 24 & 25, Application No. 21.
Today
Thamayanthi Boutique connects you directly to these artisans — preserving this heritage, one saree at a time.
